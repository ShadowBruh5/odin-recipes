Ramen

Description

Ramen is a basic but tasty meal from Japan but has since became very popular all around the globe.

Ingridients

  • 2 cups of water
  • pack of noodles
  • Ramen flavoring seasoning

Instructions

  1. Pour 2 cups of water into a pot
  2. Break noodles into four and put in pot
  3. Put the seasoning into pot.
  4. Put the stove inbetween high and medium
  5. Let noodles cook until 3 minutes of boiling
  6. Pour into bowl wait 5 minutes and then enjoy
